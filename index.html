INtroduction

Welcome to the publication wing of the PIEAS Society of Physics (PSP). We are dedicated to share knowledge, research, and insights within the physics community. Our mission is to create a platform for academic excellence and foster intellectual discourse among students and researchers.
Through our carefully curated publications, we aim to showcase the latest developments in physics, highlight groundbreaking research, and provide a space for the academic community to engage with cutting-edge ideas. Each publication is crafted with meticulous attention to detail and professional standards. Our publications serve as a bridge between academia and the broader scientific community, ensuring that important discoveries and insights reach those who need them most. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of academic integrity and presentation.

HORIZON

Issue 1 | 2025

This inauguaral issue of horizon is a celebration of quantum science, discovery, and curiosity, featuring expert articles, student writing, science history, and creative expressions.


Key Highlights:

  • 100 years of Quantum Mechanics explored in depth.
  • Article on String Theory.
  • Student pieces on dark matter, wormholes & the multiverse.

ZENITH

Zenith is our quarterly newsletter bringing you the latest physics news, community events, and highlights from the academic world.

Zenith - 2022

Issue 1 | July 2022

The inaugural bimonthly issue encompasses various subfields from the atom to the cosmos, specifically focusing on building a culture of physics and mathematics.


Key Highlights:

  • A tribute to Dr. Suhail Zubairy for his pioneering contributions to quantum optics and laser physics.
  • Coverage of the first ever image of the black hole at the center of the Milky Way.
  • Student project on Photon Blockade in Cavity QED for single photon generation.
